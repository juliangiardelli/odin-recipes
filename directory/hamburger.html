Hamburger

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions:

  1. Form the ground beef into patties.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your liking.
  3. Toast the buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Assemble the burger with your choice of toppings.
